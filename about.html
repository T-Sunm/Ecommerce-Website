Who We Are?

Cara was born with the desire to become a typical model of RESPONSIBILITY ENTERPRISES by doing both methodical and profitable business and at the same time bringing practical and long-term values to customers, employees, for partners, for the community, for society and for shareholders

Cara founded in March 2019, Nhu (CEO), Hiep (CTO) and Lan (CMO) started with a 20m2 warehouse, and a stub sales website, with very basic products for men. such as t-shirts, socks and underwear.

After 3 years we also have nearly 2000m2 of offices and warehouses operating in Danang and Ho Chi Minh. The Cara family also has nearly 100 members. The number of orders that Cara has processed up to now has reached nearly 1 million.
